Exercise in Song Medical

Mr S. wants to go home.
He says that he’s “fed up of the ward.”
Reacted well to SSRIs early on,
Then improved of own accord.

Throughout his week-long stay,
S. got better day by day.
In conclusion, therefore, I recommend
Discharge, effective today.

I might be found a-roving over land, sea or foam
But that will not change a thing.
